### Purpose

Define the sequential end-of-day closing procedure for the spa reception so that all revenue is reconciled, systems are secured, equipment is safely shut down, and the facility is prepared for the next operating day.

### Scope

1. [S1] Applies to the closing-shift spa receptionist and any team member assigned to the closing duty.
2. [S2] Covers all post-final-appointment tasks from revenue reconciliation through facility lockdown.

### Responsibility

1. [R1] **Closing-Shift Spa Receptionist** — executes the full closing sequence.
2. [R2] **Spa Reception Manager** — verifies closing accuracy on next business day.

### Procedure

**Revenue & Reporting**

1. [P1] Run the end-of-day revenue reconciliation on `[POS SYSTEM]`. Verify all transactions balance.
2. [P2] Generate the daily report from `[SPA RESERVATION SYSTEM]` and route it to Finance.
3. [P3] Prepare bills and pre-stage paperwork for next-day appointments.
4. [P4] Pull the next-day therapist schedule from `[SPA RESERVATION SYSTEM]` and place at the reception desk.

**Document & Filing**

5. [P5] File all completed paperwork from the day (consultation cards, receipts, signed forms) in the designated reception filing system.

**Front-of-House Teardown**

6. [P6] Clean and store the tea service and beverage station. Dispose of any perishable items.
7. [P7] Prepare cold towels for the next day and store appropriately.

**Equipment Shut-Down**

8. [P8] Shut down all heating equipment — towel warmers, oil warmers, hot stone warmers, hot stone beds, massage bed heating. Verify each unit is fully powered off.
9. [P9] Do not adjust the ambient music system — it is pre-programmed to end at the designated closing time.

**Locker Room Next-Day Setup**

10. [P10] Complete next-day setup for the Fitness Locker Room (restock towels, reset lockers, replenish amenities).
11. [P11] Complete next-day setup for the Spa Locker Room (same tasks as above).

**Retail & Facility Security**

12. [P12] Lock the retail display area and verify all retail inventory is secure.
13. [P13] Power off all computers and `[POS SYSTEM]` terminals.

**Final Lockdown**

14. [P14] Lock the main spa door.
15. [P15] Deliver the spa key to Security.

**Standing Hygiene Rule**

Used equipment and devices must be disinfected every 15 minutes throughout the operating day, including the final session. Confirm all surfaces are disinfected before lockdown.

### Checklist

1. [C1] `[POS SYSTEM]` revenue reconciliation completed and balanced.
2. [C2] Daily report generated and sent to Finance.
3. [C3] Next-day schedule pulled and placed at reception.
4. [C4] Day's paperwork filed.
5. [C5] Tea service cleaned and cold towels prepared.
6. [C6] All heating equipment powered off.
7. [C7] Locker rooms set up for next day.
8. [C8] Retail area locked.
9. [C9] Computers and `[POS SYSTEM]` powered off.
10. [C10] Main door locked; key delivered to Security.

### Brand notes

Closing time may vary by property and season. Adjust the sequence timing accordingly; the steps and their order remain the same.
