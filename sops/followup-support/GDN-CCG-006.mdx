## Purpose

Ensure every spa payment is processed accurately, securely, and smoothly across all accepted payment methods — card (chip, magstripe, contactless/NFC), mobile wallet, and guest-folio settlement — and that the bill is clearly itemized and presented professionally. [LQA 21.39]

## Scope

1. [S1] Applies to all spa reception staff who process payments at the `[POS SYSTEM]`.
2. [S2] Covers chip/magstripe card transactions, NFC/contactless payments, mobile wallet payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay, etc.), and guest-folio (room charge) settlement.
3. [S3] Cash handling, if applicable, follows the property's general cashier SOP and is outside this procedure.

## Responsibility

1. [R1] **Spa Receptionist** — processes all payments per this SOP.
2. [R2] **Spa Reception Manager** — reconciles daily POS reports and resolves discrepancies.
3. [R3] **Finance Department** — receives end-of-day POS paperwork.

## Procedure

**Bill Preparation**

1. [P1] Before presenting the bill, verify all line items are correct — treatment(s), enhancements, retail purchases, applicable fees. The bill must be clearly itemized. [LQA 21.39]
2. [P2] Present the bill in a clean billfold (or similar presenter) that is in good condition. [LQA 21.39]

**Chip / Magstripe Card Payment**

3. [P3] For chip cards: insert the card into the chip reader per the terminal instructions. For magstripe cards: swipe with the magnetic stripe facing down per the terminal instructions.
4. [P4] Verify the card number and validity on the `[POS SYSTEM]` screen. Press the confirm button to proceed.
5. [P5] Enter the exact amount (e.g., 500.00 TL). Confirm the amount is correct before finalizing. If incorrect, cancel and re-enter.
6. [P6] After the receipt prints, confirm the card number and amount once more.
7. [P7] If the terminal prompts for a signature: present the receipt to the guest for signature, then verify the signature matches the card's signature panel. If the signature panel is unsigned or absent, request a photo ID matching the name on the card.
8. [P8] Provide the guest copy of the receipt to the guest.

**NFC / Contactless Payment**

9. [P9] Activate the contactless reader on the `[POS SYSTEM]` terminal and enter the transaction amount.
10. [P10] Invite the guest to tap their card or device on the reader. Confirm the transaction completes successfully.
11. [P11] Provide the guest copy of the receipt (printed or offered digitally, per guest preference).

**Mobile Wallet Payment (Apple Pay, Google Pay, etc.)**

12. [P12] Process identically to NFC/contactless (steps P.9–P.11). The terminal treats mobile wallet taps the same as contactless card taps.

**Guest-Folio (Room Charge) Settlement**

13. [P13] Confirm the guest's name and room number. Verify against the hotel PMS that the room allows posting.
14. [P14] Post the charge to the guest folio in `[POS SYSTEM]`. Obtain the guest's signature on the folio charge slip.
15. [P15] Provide the guest a copy of the signed charge slip.

**End-of-Transaction & Filing**

16. [P16] Retain the merchant copy and any additional copies. File together with the daily finance paperwork.
17. [P17] Route all completed POS paperwork to Finance at the end of the shift. [Cross-ref SOP-GDN-CCG-005.P.1]

<Accordion title="Checklist" icon="clipboard-check">
1. [C1] Bill clearly itemized and verified before presenting.
2. [C2] Bill presented in clean billfold / presenter.
3. [C3] Card number and amount verified on screen.
4. [C4] Signature verified (or ID checked if signature panel absent).
5. [C5] Guest copy provided.
6. [C6] Paperwork filed and routed to Finance.
</Accordion>
