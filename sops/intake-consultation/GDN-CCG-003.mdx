### Purpose

Define the unified procedure for modifying, cancelling, or handling no-shows for spa appointments — including fee tiers, guest communication standards, and recovery protocols — so that every schedule change is handled with positive language and transparent policy enforcement.

### Scope

1. [S1] Applies to all spa reception staff, spa coordinators, and duty managers involved in appointment modifications, cancellations, or no-show follow-up.
2. [S2] Covers telephone, in-person, and digital change/cancellation requests, plus no-show recovery.
3. [S3] Cancellation policy must be communicated at time of booking. [LQA 21.7 — cross-ref SOP-GDN-CCG-002.P.18]

### Responsibility

1. [R1] **Spa Receptionist** — processes changes, applies fee tiers, executes no-show protocol.
2. [R2] **Spa Reception Manager / Spa Supervisor** — reviews no-show charges, approves fee waivers.
3. [R3] **Spa Director** — approves any changes to cancellation fee tiers or waiver policy.

### Procedure

**A — Appointment Changes**

1. [P1] If a guest calls to change a treatment, always try to accommodate the guest's wishes.
2. [P2] Never act annoyed or impatient with the guest due to a change in their schedule. Always use affirmative, positive language. Mandated phrasing: *"Absolutely Mr./Mrs. [GUEST NAME], I would be happy to assist you with that change. What time were you thinking?"*
3. [P3] Check that the therapist assigned to the original booking is qualified to perform the newly requested treatment. If not, inform the guest of the therapist change.
4. [P4] If a specific therapist was requested, verify that therapist is available at the newly requested time slot.
5. [P5] Inform the guest of any resulting changes to: appointment time, treatment price, or treatment room.
6. [P6] If the guest requests a treatment change upon arrival at the spa, always attempt to accommodate. If necessary, the treatment time may be slightly adjusted to allow therapists to prepare the treatment room. If the change is impossible due to scheduling or therapist qualifications, apologize and proceed with the existing booking.
7. [P7] Update the booking in `[SPA RESERVATION SYSTEM]` immediately after confirming the change with the guest.

**B — Cancellation Fee Tiers**

8. [P8] Cancellation fee schedule:
   a. [SOP-GDN-CCG-003.P.8a] **More than 6 hours before the appointment:** cancellation is free.
   b. [SOP-GDN-CCG-003.P.8b] **Between 48 hours and 6 hours before:** 50% cancellation fee.
   c. [SOP-GDN-CCG-003.P.8c] **Less than 6 hours before:** 100% cancellation fee.
   d. [SOP-GDN-CCG-003.P.8d] For package treatments cancelled less than 6 hours before, the package is deducted as if performed.

9. [P9] Communicate the applicable fee clearly and empathetically when processing a cancellation.

**C — Day-Before Confirmation**

10. [P10] A day-before confirmation call (D-1) is mandatory for every booked treatment. Confirm the treatment type, time, and any preparation instructions.

**D — No-Show Protocol**

11. [P11] If a guest does not arrive for their appointment, call the guest assuming a time confusion — never assume abandonment.
12. [P12] If the guest is reached and a misunderstanding is confirmed, reschedule at the earliest mutually convenient slot.
13. [P13] If the guest cannot be reached, note the guest file with a timestamp and caller initials.
14. [P14] Waive the no-show fee where there is a valid reason or where the error is on the spa's side.
15. [P15] Charge the no-show fee where no valid reason exists.
16. [P16] Print the receipt, attach a brief written explanation, route to the Spa Supervisor, and add a system note to the guest profile in `[SPA RESERVATION SYSTEM]`.

### Checklist

1. [C1] Affirmative language used for all change requests.
2. [C2] Therapist qualification verified before reassignment.
3. [C3] Correct cancellation fee tier applied.
4. [C4] D-1 confirmation call made for all bookings.
5. [C5] No-show guest contacted (assume time confusion).
6. [C6] Booking system updated after every change, cancellation, or no-show.
7. [C7] No-show receipt printed and routed to Spa Supervisor.

### Brand notes

Cancellation fee tiers and waiver policies may be adjusted per property. Any deviation from the standard tiers above must be documented in a property-specific addendum.
