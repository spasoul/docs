## Purpose

Ensure every spa reservation — whether by telephone, walk-in, or online — is captured accurately, the guest receives a complete pre-visit briefing, and at least one upsell opportunity is presented during the booking interaction.

## Scope

1. [S1] Applies to all spa reception staff and spa coordinators who accept or process spa reservations.
2. [S2] Covers telephone bookings, walk-in bookings, and online/digital booking follow-up.
3. [S3] Online booking platform usability and configuration are outside this SOP but must meet the "easy to navigate and book" standard. [LQA 21.2]

## Responsibility

1. [R1] **Spa Receptionist** — executes the full booking procedure per this SOP.
2. [R2] **Spa Reception Manager** — monitors booking quality, upsell conversion, and system accuracy.
3. [R3] **Spa Director** — owns the treatment menu and approves upsell offerings.

## Procedure

**Guest Engagement**

1. [P1] Greet the guest with a smile and introduce yourself by first name. [LQA 21.1 — cross-ref SOP-GDN-CCG-001]
2. [P2] Offer assistance and ask for the guest's name. **Use the guest's name at least two (2) times** during the booking interaction. [LQA 21.6]
3. [P3] For walk-in guests: invite the guest to be seated in the Relaxation Lounge and offer the treatment menu.
4. [P4] Offer the welcome beverage at the Relaxation Lounge.

**Treatment Selection & Upsell**

5. [P5] Present treatment choices. Be knowledgeable about all treatments and spa facilities; answer any questions confidently. [LQA 21.5]
6. [P6] Ask about massage preferences (pressure, focus areas, oil/scent preferences).
7. [P7] Recommend treatments and **attempt to upsell at least one in-treatment enhancement** (e.g., extended duration, add-on service, product upgrade).
8. [P8] Clarify the exact treatment required — type and duration. [LQA 21.3]
9. [P9] If the booking is made within 24 hours: confirm the therapist's gender preference at the time of booking. Record this preference and ensure it is adhered to. [LQA 21.4]

**Health & Scheduling**

10. [P10] Ask about any health conditions, allergies, or contraindications. Direct the guest to complete the health consultation form.
11. [P11] Explain the treatment session time (including any buffer time before/after).
12. [P12] Confirm the preferred appointment date and time.
13. [P13] Confirm the number of guests for the booking.
14. [P14] Obtain the guest's room number and/or contact number. [LQA 21.6]

**Confirmation & Pre-Visit Briefing**

15. [P15] Provide an appointment card (physical or digital).
16. [P16] Repeat the full reservation details back to the guest — treatment type, duration, date, time, therapist gender (if specified), and number of guests. [LQA 21.6]
17. [P17] Explain charges clearly.
18. [P18] Explain the late cancellation and no-show fee policy. [LQA 21.7 — cross-ref SOP-GDN-CCG-003]
19. [P19] Advise the guest what time to arrive for the treatment (recommended arrival time before the scheduled session). [LQA 21.8]
20. [P20] Inform the guest about spa wear etiquette and what to expect upon arrival.
21. [P21] Promote spa facilities if available (pool, sauna, steam room, relaxation areas). [LQA 21.8]
22. [P22] Send a booking confirmation that includes spa etiquette and cancellation policy. [LQA 21.7]

**Closing**

23. [P23] Ask: *"Is there anything else I can assist you with at this stage?"*
24. [P24] Thank the guest.

**Walk-In with Existing Reservation**

25. [P25] Confirm the guest's name and treatment details against the booking record.
26. [P26] Ask the guest to complete the health consultation card (if not already on file).
27. [P27] Escort the guest to the Relaxation Lounge and hand over to the arrival process. [Cross-ref SOP-GDN-CUR-001]

**System Entry**

28. [P28] Enter all booking details and guest notes into `[SPA RESERVATION SYSTEM]` immediately after the interaction.

<Accordion title="Checklist" icon="clipboard-check">
1. [C1] Guest name obtained and used at least twice.
2. [C2] Treatment type and duration clarified and confirmed.
3. [C3] Therapist gender confirmed (if booking within 24 hours).
4. [C4] Health conditions / allergies asked.
5. [C5] Upsell attempted (at least one enhancement offered).
6. [C6] Reservation details repeated back to guest.
7. [C7] Cancellation policy and charges explained.
8. [C8] Arrival time and spa etiquette communicated.
9. [C9] Confirmation sent with spa etiquette and cancellation policy.
10. [C10] Booking entered into `[SPA RESERVATION SYSTEM]`.
</Accordion>
