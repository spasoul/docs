## Purpose

Define the procedure for booking, redeeming, and reconciling spa gift certificates and vouchers — including handling missing or lost certificates — to ensure accurate financial controls and a seamless guest experience.

## Scope

1. [S1] Applies to all spa reception staff who process gift certificate bookings or redemptions.
2. [S2] Covers in-person redemption, phone/digital booking with certificate reference, and lost-certificate recovery.
3. [S3] Gift certificate sales/issuance follow the property's retail/sales SOP and are outside this procedure.

## Responsibility

1. [R1] **Spa Receptionist** — processes redemptions and collects documentation per this SOP.
2. [R2] **Spa Reception Manager** — reconciles the gift certificate bucket and resolves discrepancies.
3. [R3] **Finance Department** — performs final reconciliation of redeemed certificates.

## Procedure

**Booking with Gift Certificate**

1. [P1] At booking, review the gift certificate details: certificate number, value, expiry date, and purchaser name.
2. [P2] Re-explain the cancellation policy to the certificate recipient (the recipient may not be the original purchaser). [Cross-ref SOP-GDN-CCG-003]

**Standard Redemption (Certificate Present)**

3. [P3] Collect the gift certificate from the guest upon arrival.
4. [P4] Print the treatment ticket and highlight the certificate number on the ticket.
5. [P5] Upon treatment completion, have the guest sign the treatment ticket.
6. [P6] Route the signed ticket and the original certificate to the Manager's designated gift certificate reconciliation file.

**Guest Arrives Without Certificate**

7. [P7] Charge the guest's credit card on file as a placeholder transaction.
8. [P8] Issue a full refund to the credit card once the original certificate is received and verified.
9. [P9] Document the charge-then-refund in the guest profile and the daily POS reconciliation.

**Lost Certificate Recovery**

10. [P10] Verify the certificate by cross-referencing: date of purchase, original purchase amount, purchaser name.
11. [P11] Request valid photo ID from the guest and confirm the name matches the purchaser or authorized recipient.
12. [P12] Obtain the guest's signature for verification.
13. [P13] If all verification checks pass, honor the certificate. If any check fails, escalate to the Spa Reception Manager before proceeding.

**Post-Redemption**

14. [P14] Update the guest profile notes with the redemption details (certificate number, date redeemed, treatment provided).

<Accordion title="Checklist" icon="clipboard-check">
1. [C1] Certificate details reviewed at booking (number, value, expiry, purchaser).
2. [C2] Cancellation policy explained to recipient.
3. [C3] Treatment ticket printed with certificate number highlighted.
4. [C4] Guest signature obtained on treatment ticket.
5. [C5] Signed ticket routed to reconciliation file.
6. [C6] Charge-then-refund documented (if applicable).
7. [C7] Guest profile updated with redemption.
</Accordion>


<Accordion title="Brand Notes" icon="building">
Properties may use digital gift certificate platforms that automate verification and redemption tracking. The manual verification steps (P.10–P.13) remain the fallback for system outages or physical certificates.
</Accordion>
