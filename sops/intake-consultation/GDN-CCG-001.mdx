## Purpose

Establish uniform standards for answering telephone calls, acknowledging walk-in guests, and responding to digital communications at the spa reception, ensuring every guest interaction reflects modern luxury service from the first point of contact.

## Scope

1. [S1] Applies to all spa reception staff, spa coordinators, and any team member who answers the spa telephone line or responds to spa-related digital inquiries.
2. [S2] Covers telephone calls, in-person greetings at the spa reception desk, and digital communications (email, messaging platforms).
3. [S3] Excludes inter-departmental internal calls (covered by property-wide communication standards).

## Responsibility

1. [R1] **Spa Receptionist** — answers all incoming calls and greets walk-in guests per this SOP.
2. [R2] **Spa Reception Manager** — monitors adherence, conducts spot-checks, and updates scripts as needed.
3. [R3] **Spa Director** — approves any changes to mandated greeting/closing scripts.

## Procedure

**Telephone Calls**

1. [P1] Answer all incoming calls within **three (3) rings or less**. [LQA 21.1]
2. [P2] Use the standard greeting verbatim: *"Good Morning/Afternoon/Evening, [PROPERTY SPA NAME]. This is [FIRST NAME]."* Always identify yourself to the caller.
3. [P3] Smile while speaking — a smiling face produces a happy voice. Speak slowly. Give the caller your full attention.
4. [P4] If the caller must be placed on hold, ask permission first. **Never keep a guest waiting for longer than 30 seconds without acknowledging them.** [LQA 21.1 — note: LQA allows 90 seconds for in-person acknowledgment; this SOP applies the stricter 30-second standard for all channels.]
5. [P5] Close every call with the standard farewell verbatim: *"Thank you for calling [PROPERTY SPA NAME]. Have a pleasant day/afternoon/evening."*

**Walk-In Guests**

6. [P6] Acknowledge every guest who enters the spa reception area within **30 seconds** — make eye contact, smile, and offer a verbal greeting. [LQA 21.1]
7. [P7] If engaged with another guest or on the telephone, acknowledge the waiting guest with eye contact and a nod, and attend to them as soon as possible within the 30-second window.

**Digital Communications**

8. [P8] Respond to all digital communications (email, messaging platforms) within **3 minutes** during operating hours. [LQA 21.1]
9. [P9] Use the same professional tone as telephone interactions. Include your name and the spa name in the sign-off.

**Things Never to Say**

10. [P10] Never reveal unfamiliarity with treatments offered at the spa. If unsure, say: *"Allow me to confirm the details for you — I will be right back."*
11. [P11] Never reveal operational issues to a guest (e.g., "We don't have any therapist available"). Instead, offer the nearest available alternative.
12. [P12] Never discuss personal problems or express a negative mood to a guest.

<Accordion title="Checklist" icon="clipboard-check">
1. [C1] Phone answered within 3 rings.
2. [C2] Standard greeting used verbatim (including first name).
3. [C3] Guest acknowledged within 30 seconds (all channels).
4. [C4] Standard farewell used verbatim.
5. [C5] Digital communication responded to within 3 minutes.
6. [C6] No prohibited phrases used during interaction.
</Accordion>
