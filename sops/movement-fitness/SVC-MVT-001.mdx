### Purpose

Define the responsibilities, circulation schedule, service behaviours, and guest interaction standards for Fitness Centre attendants during all operating hours, ensuring the gym floor is actively maintained and guests receive confident, anticipatory service.

### Scope

1. [S1] Applies to all Fitness Attendants and Gym Supervisors on duty during gym operating hours.
2. [S2] Covers the full gym floor operating period from opening to closing (or continuously for 24-hour access properties).
3. [S3] Applies regardless of gym occupancy level — standards do not reduce during quiet periods.
4. [S4] Facility setup and opening checklist are covered in SOP-OPR-FAC-001. This SOP governs in-shift service delivery only.

### Responsibility

1. [R1] **Gym Attendant** — executes circulation rounds per schedule, delivers guest services, maintains cleanliness between rounds, reports defects immediately.
2. [R2] **Fitness Centre Manager / Gym Supervisor** — monitors round compliance, handles escalated guest requests, ensures class/PT schedule is current.

### Procedure

**Attendant Circulation**

1. [P1] When staff are present in the gym, **actively circulate the full gym floor at least every 20 minutes**, making yourself readily available to guests. [Forbes Fitness 5.1]
   > **Auditor note:** LQA 19.4 specifies a 30-minute walk-through for *unattended* gyms between 08:00 and 20:00. Forbes requires 20-minute active circulation when staff are present. This SOP adopts Forbes 20 minutes as the property-wide in-shift standard (plan §4 stricter-wins rule). Both standards are cited in the compliance matrix for full audit traceability.
2. [P2] If the gym is **unattended** during any period (e.g., brief staff absence), an employee must walk through the gym **every 30 minutes between 08:00 and 20:00** to ensure it is kept tidy and sufficiently stocked. [LQA 19.4]
3. [P3] During each circulation round, complete the following in sequence:
   a. [SOP-SVC-MVT-001.P.3a] Observe guest activity without interrupting unless service or safety requires it.
   b. [SOP-SVC-MVT-001.P.3b] Collect and discreetly remove used or soiled towels — never leave soiled towels visible to other guests.
   c. [SOP-SVC-MVT-001.P.3c] Replenish depleted amenity stations: sweat towels, chilled towels, water cups, disinfectant wipes, fruit/healthy snacks.
   d. [SOP-SVC-MVT-001.P.3d] Wipe down any equipment vacated by a guest using approved disinfectant solution.
   e. [SOP-SVC-MVT-001.P.3e] Clear any debris, equipment accessories, or personal items left on machines or the floor.
   f. [SOP-SVC-MVT-001.P.3f] Log any equipment fault or defect discovered during the round in the [MAINTENANCE LOG] and notify Engineering if not already reported. → See SOP-OPR-FAC-001 P.20.

**Guest Interaction**

4. [P4] When not circulating, position yourself at the attendant station to remain visible and accessible to guests.
5. [P5] Greet each arriving guest warmly. Offer to orient first-time users to the gym layout, equipment zones, and available amenities.
6. [P6] Be professional and courteous at all times. Project confidence and knowledge — if asked a question you cannot answer immediately, offer to find out rather than declining. [Forbes Fitness 5.1]
7. [P7] Anticipate guest needs — if a guest appears to be looking for something (towel, water, assistance), approach proactively rather than waiting to be asked. [Forbes Fitness 5.1 cross-cutting service-tone]
8. [P8] Do not decline a request without offering an alternative. If a guest's request cannot be met (e.g., equipment temporarily out of order, class fully booked), propose the nearest available equivalent. [Forbes Fitness 5.1 cross-cutting]
9. [P9] Use the guest's name as a form of recognition when known and appropriate — do not overuse. [Forbes Fitness 5.1 cross-cutting]
10. [P10] Maintain alert posture, make eye contact, smile, and maintain a gracious, polite tone at all times on the gym floor. Articulate clearly and speak in a manner easily understood. [Forbes Fitness 5.1 cross-cutting service-tone]
11. [P11] Offer a polite closing to each guest interaction or when a guest is departing the gym. [Forbes Fitness 5.1 cross-cutting]

**Fitness Class & Personal Training Promotion**

12. [P12] Ensure the [FITNESS CLASS & PERSONAL TRAINING SCHEDULE] is always current, clearly displayed at [SCHEDULE DISPLAY LOCATION], and — where applicable — listed in the in-room compendium or fitness centre reception. [LQA 19.10]
13. [P13] Proactively make guests aware of fitness class and/or personal training availability, particularly for guests who appear to be seeking guidance, are first-time users, or ask about fitness programming. [LQA 19.10; Forbes Fitness 5.1]
14. [P14] When promoting classes or PT sessions, provide accurate information on duration, level, and instructor. Direct the guest to the schedule or to [CONTACT POINT FOR BOOKINGS].

**Headphone Service**

15. [P15] Complimentary headphones are available to guests at [HEADPHONE STATION LOCATION] or on request from the attendant. [LQA 19.12]
16. [P16] Before presenting headphones to a guest, verify: [LQA 19.12]
    a. [SOP-SVC-MVT-001.P.16a] Headphones are clean and in good working order.
    b. [SOP-SVC-MVT-001.P.16b] Ear pieces are hygienic — sealed/new liner or sanitised per the property sanitation protocol.
17. [P17] At the end of a guest's session, collect used headphones. Clean, sanitise ear pieces, test function, and return to the station ready for the next guest. [LQA 19.12]
18. [P18] Any headphone unit that is faulty or cannot be sanitised must be removed from service. Notify the Fitness Centre Manager for replacement.

### Checklist

1. [C1] Circulation round completed on schedule: every 20 minutes (attended) / every 30 minutes 08:00–20:00 (unattended).
2. [C2] Used/soiled towels removed discreetly during each round; stations restocked.
3. [C3] Equipment wiped down after guest use during each round.
4. [C4] Any equipment fault discovered during round logged and Engineering notified.
5. [C5] Headphones sanitised and function-tested before each presentation.
6. [C6] Fitness class/PT schedule current and displayed at designated location.
7. [C7] All guest requests resolved or alternative offered — no request declined without an alternative.

### Brand Notes

- **No legacy SOP baseline.** Authored from scratch against LQA 19.4, 19.10, 19.12, and Forbes Fitness 5.1 cross-cutting service-tone block. No Template-SOPs.md equivalent exists for this stream.
- **Forbes 20 min vs. LQA 30 min:** Forbes 5.1 requires active 20-minute circulation for attended gyms; LQA 19.4 specifies 30 minutes for unattended. Stricter-wins rule (plan §4) → Forbes 20 min adopted for the in-shift standard. Both cited in compliance matrix. Auditors evaluating LQA 19.4 conformance should accept Forbes 20-min compliance as satisfying the 30-min standard.
- **[FITNESS CLASS & PERSONAL TRAINING SCHEDULE]**, **[SCHEDULE DISPLAY LOCATION]**, **[CONTACT POINT FOR BOOKINGS]**, **[HEADPHONE STATION LOCATION]** — all property-specific placeholders to be replaced at rollout.
- **[MAINTENANCE LOG]** — property specifies platform/format. Cross-reference to SOP-OPR-FAC-001.
- **Forbes cross-cutting service-tone block:** steps P.6–P.11 are derived from Forbes 5.1 universal service-tone standards (politeness, eye contact, name use, anticipation, polite closing, no-decline rule). These apply equally across all Fitness SOPs wherever guest-facing service occurs.
