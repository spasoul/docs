## Purpose

To assess sleep quality and deliver personalised sleep optimisation strategies — covering circadian rhythm alignment, sleep hygiene education, environment design, and technology-assisted sleep tracking — to support recovery, cognitive performance, and longevity outcomes.

---

## Scope

Covers sleep assessment (subjective and wearable-based), sleep environment audit, personalised sleep protocol design, and coaching during programme stay. Does not cover diagnosis or treatment of clinical sleep disorders (obstructive sleep apnoea, narcolepsy, chronic insomnia — separate sleep medicine referral required).

---

## Responsibility

[Coach] — certified sleep coach or wellness professional; conducts assessments, designs sleep protocols, and delivers coaching. Physician referral required if clinical sleep disorder suspected (e.g., AHI >5 on screening, chronic insomnia >3 months, excessive daytime sleepiness with safety concern).


<Warning>
**Absolute Contraindications** — Do not proceed under any circumstances:
- None for sleep coaching itself; however, any sleep-related pharmacological intervention requires physician prescription and is outside this SOP's scope.
</Warning>


<Note>
**Relative Contraindications** — Physician clearance required:
- Suspected obstructive sleep apnoea (screening questionnaire score above threshold — refer for polysomnography)
- Chronic insomnia with psychiatric comorbidity (depression, anxiety, PTSD — CBT-I may require clinical psychologist)
- Shift-work or jet-lag disorder requiring pharmacological support
- Use of sleep medication or sedatives (protocol must account for medication interactions)
</Note>
