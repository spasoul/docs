## Purpose

To deliver personalised mindfulness and stress management coaching — covering meditation, body scan, journaling, cognitive reframing, and HRV-guided stress awareness — to support autonomic regulation, emotional resilience, cortisol management, and overall wellbeing as part of the longevity programme.

---

## Scope

Covers mindfulness assessment, personalised stress management protocol design, guided meditation/mindfulness sessions (individual and small-group), and take-home practice guidance. Does not cover psychotherapy, clinical treatment of anxiety/depression/PTSD, or pharmacological intervention (separate clinical referral required).

---

## Responsibility

[Coach] — certified mindfulness facilitator or wellness professional; conducts assessments, designs protocols, and leads sessions. Physician or clinical psychologist referral required if significant psychiatric symptoms (panic attacks, dissociation, suicidal ideation) are identified.


<Warning>
**Absolute Contraindications** — Do not proceed under any circumstances:
- Active psychosis or acute psychiatric crisis
- Recent trauma exposure where meditation may trigger re-experiencing (within [X weeks] — clinical assessment required first)
</Warning>


<Note>
**Relative Contraindications** — Physician clearance required:
- PTSD or complex trauma history (meditation may induce dissociation or flashbacks — trauma-informed approach required)
- Severe depression with active suicidal ideation (clinical care priority)
- Dissociative disorders (body scan and meditation may exacerbate)
- Active substance abuse (stress management coaching must be integrated with recovery programme)
</Note>
