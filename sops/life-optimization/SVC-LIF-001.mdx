## Purpose

To deliver personalised nutrition guidance — covering macronutrient balance, micronutrient optimisation, meal timing, and evidence-based dietary strategies (Mediterranean, time-restricted eating, anti-inflammatory protocols) — to support metabolic health, body composition, and longevity outcomes.

---

## Scope

Covers initial nutritional assessment, personalised meal plan design, ongoing dietary coaching during the programme stay, and take-home nutrition guidance. Does not cover clinical medical nutrition therapy for diagnosed conditions (diabetes, renal disease, eating disorders — separate clinical referral required), nor F&B kitchen operations.

---

## Responsibility

[Coach] — certified nutritionist or registered dietitian; conducts assessments, designs meal plans, delivers coaching sessions, and coordinates with F&B team on guest dietary requirements. Physician referral required if disordered eating, metabolic disease, or food allergy/anaphylaxis risk identified.


<Warning>
**Absolute Contraindications** — Do not proceed under any circumstances:
- Active eating disorder (anorexia, bulimia — requires clinical care, not wellness coaching)
- Severe food allergy with anaphylaxis history (meal planning only with allergist clearance and epi-pen protocol in place)
</Warning>


<Note>
**Relative Contraindications** — Physician clearance required:
- Diabetes (Type 1 or insulin-dependent Type 2 — dietary changes affect glycaemic control)
- Chronic kidney disease (protein/electrolyte restrictions)
- Pregnancy or breastfeeding (caloric restriction or fasting protocols contraindicated)
- Current use of warfarin or other medications with significant food interactions
- History of disordered eating (sub-clinical — coaching approach must be adapted)
</Note>
