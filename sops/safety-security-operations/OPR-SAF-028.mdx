### Purpose

Establish the requirements for selecting associates and contracted drivers who transport guests or associates in motor vehicles, vans, or courtesy vehicles, and for controlling the use of transportation vehicles on [the property].

---

### Scope

All departments. All associates and contracted personnel who operate guest or company vehicles, or who transport guests or associates.

---

### Responsibility

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| [Occupational Safety Manager] | Oversight of driver safety compliance |
| [Human Resources Manager] | Maintain licensing documentation in personnel files; receive and verify driver documents |
| Finance Department | Receive and verify vehicle records; check insurance and inspection document validity on receipt and for vehicle changes |
| Department Heads | Provide names and documents of personnel likely to drive to Security and HR; ensure Vehicle User Lists are current |
| Security Department | Monitor and control vehicle users against Vehicle User Lists; receive allocated vehicles on associate off-days |
| All driving associates / contracted drivers | Hold valid, current licensing; submit required documents; complete Daily Vehicle Control Form |

---

### Procedure

#### Section A — Licensing and Documentation

**1.** [P1] **Licensing compliance** — [The property] must maintain licensing documentation in compliance with all applicable local rules, regulations, and requirements.

**2.** [P2] **Associate driver documentation** — Each associate who transports guests or other associates, or who operates a guest or company vehicle, must review and sign the Hotel Vehicle or Service Vehicle Usage Instruction (Driver Responsibility Form). This document must be placed in the associate's personnel file.

**3.** [P3] **Documents required for driving roles** — Individuals applying for positions that include transporting guests or associates and/or operating guest or company vehicles must provide: a valid driver's licence (accepted in Turkey and appropriate for the vehicle type). Documents must be kept in the personnel file in Human Resources.

**4.** [P4] **SRC certificate** — Associates without an SRC (vocational competence) certificate may not drive a commercial vehicle.

---

#### Section B — Contracted Transportation Services

**5.** [P5] **Contracted firm documentation** — Contracted Transportation Services Firms providing vehicles and drivers must supply: a valid driver's licence (accepted in Turkey and appropriate for vehicle type); a passenger carrier licence (required if transporting passengers for a fee); a signed and stamped Hotel Vehicle or Service Vehicle Usage Instruction from both the driver and the authorised person of the contracted firm.

**6.** [P6] **Document updates** — Documentation must be updated whenever a vehicle or driver changes. The Department Manager concerned is responsible for ensuring the update. Employee information must be submitted to HR for the subcontractor folder; vehicle records must be submitted to Finance.

**7.** [P7] **Contracted firm obligations** — The contracted firm is responsible for the execution and follow-up of all periodic maintenance, insurance, and vehicle inspection requirements. These obligations must be stated in the contract. Finance must check document dates and validity on receipt and upon vehicle changes.

---

#### Section C — Daily Operations and Vehicle Control

**8.** [P8] **Daily vehicle check** — Each vehicle must be checked daily by the driver using the Daily Vehicle Control Form. The completed form must be delivered to the Department Supervisor.

**9.** [P9] **Vehicle User List** — Vehicles may only be used by persons on the Vehicle User List. Security monitors and controls vehicle use against these lists.

**10.** [P10] **Vehicle handover on off-days** — Vehicles allocated to associates must be delivered to the Security Department on their off-days, except where the General Manager has specifically granted permission otherwise.

**11.** [P11] **Department Head responsibility** — Department Heads must provide the names and documents of all personnel likely to drive a vehicle to both the Security Department and Human Resources.

---

#### Section D — Guest Transportation Note

**12.** [P12] **Free guest transportation without carrier licence** — If guest transportation is performed free of charge in a vehicle that does not hold a Passenger Carrier Licence, the Guest Declaration Form must be signed by the guest. This signed form must be available for presentation during any police inspection.
