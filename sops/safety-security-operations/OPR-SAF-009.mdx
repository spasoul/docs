### Purpose

Ensure all associates know how to identify and eliminate trip hazards to prevent fall injuries.

---

### Scope

All departments. All associates working or moving anywhere on [the property].

---

### Responsibility

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| [Occupational Safety Manager] | Training on trip hazard prevention; oversee workplace tidiness standards |
| Department Heads | Reinforce procedures in work areas; ensure adequate lighting |
| All associates | Actively eliminate trip hazards; report lighting problems immediately |

---

### Procedure

**1.** [P1] **Trip hazard awareness** — Many fall injuries occur on level ground when people trip over unexpected objects in their path. All associates must actively eliminate trip hazards.

**2.** [P2] **Keep areas neat and tidy** — Keep work areas neat and tidy. Put tools, materials, and other items away after use.

**3.** [P3] **Pick up items from the floor** — Pick up items off the floor, even if you did not put them there.

**4.** [P4] **Cover cables in walkways** — Cables and cords in walkways must be covered or routed to eliminate the trip hazard.

**5.** [P5] **Navigate obstructions safely** — Step over or around obstructions — never step on them.

**6.** [P6] **Walk slowly when carrying loads** — Walk slowly and change directions slowly, especially when carrying a load.

**7.** [P7] **Watch for floor level changes** — Watch for changes in floor level such as steps or ramps, especially in unfamiliar areas.

**8.** [P8] **Report lighting problems** — Report lighting problems such as burned-out bulbs to maintenance immediately.

**9.** [P9] **Use a flashlight in the dark** — If additional light is needed when leaving a facility or working in a dark area, use a flashlight.

**10.** [P10] **Do not leave items on the floor** — Do not leave boxes, bags, tools, or other materials on the floor.

**11.** [P11] **Do not block walkways** — Do not block walkways with hand trucks, equipment, or materials.

**12.** [P12] **Do not leave cords in walkways** — Do not leave cords or cables in walkways.

**13.** [P13] **Nothing on stairs** — Do not place any object on stairs.

**14.** [P14] **Close drawers** — Do not leave drawers open.
