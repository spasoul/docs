### Purpose

Ensure all associates are protected from infectious diseases through awareness of viral illness symptoms and consistent application of prevention measures.

---

### Scope

All departments. All associates working on [the property].

---

### Responsibility

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| [Occupational Safety Manager] / [HR] | Distribute awareness materials; ensure associates are briefed on prevention measures |
| Department Heads | Reinforce compliance with prevention measures in their areas; send ill associates home |
| All associates | Follow viral illness prevention procedures at all times; report symptoms; stay home when ill |

---

### Procedure

**1.** [P1] **Awareness materials** — Awareness posters, and any materials distributed by [Human Resources] and [Loss Prevention / designated department], must be read and adhered to by all associates.

**2.** [P2] **Wear gloves** — All associates must wear gloves if they feel they may come into contact with objects or surfaces that have been contaminated.

**3.** [P3] **Wear masks** — Masks must be worn by all associates when infection conditions suggest this is prudent, or when instructed to do so.

**4.** [P4] **Ill associates stay home** — Any person observed to be showing signs of illness, or who feels they are ill, should be urged to seek medical attention. Associates who are ill must be urged to refrain from coming to work.

**5.** [P5] **Coughing and sneezing** — All persons who cough or sneeze must do so into a tissue or paper towel. These materials must be disposed of immediately and properly. Persons who touch their nose or mouth must immediately wash and disinfect their hands.

**6.** [P6] **Approved disinfectants** — Cleaning and disinfecting of areas must use only approved disinfectant agents, following correct procedures to prevent cross-contamination. Associates performing disinfecting operations must use proper personal protective equipment (PPE).
