## Purpose

Define the sequential morning opening procedure for the spa reception so that the facility, systems, and front-of-house environment are fully operational and guest-ready before the first appointment of the day.

## Scope

1. [S1] Applies to the opening-shift spa receptionist and any team member assigned to the opening duty.
2. [S2] Covers all pre-opening tasks from facility access through first-guest readiness.
3. [S3] Treatment room setup is excluded (covered by therapist pre-treatment procedures in SOP-SVC-SPA-001).

## Responsibility

1. [R1] **Opening-Shift Spa Receptionist** — executes the full opening sequence.
2. [R2] **Spa Reception Manager** — verifies opening readiness via spot-checks.

## Procedure

**Facility Access & Security**

1. [P1] Collect the spa key from Security upon arrival.
2. [P2] Unlock the main spa door. Conduct a quick walk-through to confirm no overnight anomalies (damage, leaks, security concerns).

**Systems Start-Up**

3. [P3] Power on all computers and `[POS SYSTEM]` terminals.
4. [P4] Log in to `[SPA RESERVATION SYSTEM]` and pull the day's appointment schedule. Print a copy for the reception desk and therapist station.
5. [P5] Review the day's bookings: confirm therapist assignments, flag any special requests (VIP, pregnant guest, health notes), and verify that D-1 confirmation calls were completed. [Cross-ref SOP-GDN-CCG-003.P.10]

**Heating & Equipment Start-Up**

6. [P6] Switch on all heating equipment — towel warmers, oil warmers, hot stone warmers, hot stone beds, massage bed heating — allowing adequate warm-up time before first treatment.
7. [P7] Verify the ambient music system is active. (Music is pre-programmed; do not adjust settings.)

**Front-of-House Preparation**

8. [P8] Prepare fresh tea service and welcome beverages for the Relaxation Lounge.
9. [P9] Set out fresh cold towels at the reception area.
10. [P10] Confirm the retail display area is unlocked, tidy, and fully stocked.

**Locker Room Readiness**

11. [P11] Inspect Spa Locker Room: confirm cleanliness, replenish amenities (towels, robes, slippers, toiletries), verify lockers are reset.
12. [P12] Inspect Fitness Locker Room (if shared responsibility): same checks as above.

**Hygiene & Final Checks**

13. [P13] Disinfect all guest-contact surfaces at the reception desk, Relaxation Lounge, and common areas. Used equipment and devices must be disinfected every 15 minutes throughout the operating day.
14. [P14] Review outstanding bills or carry-over items from the previous day's closing report.
15. [P15] Confirm readiness and communicate "Spa Open" status to the front desk / PBX team so guest calls can be routed.

<Accordion title="Checklist" icon="clipboard-check">
1. [C1] Spa key collected from Security.
2. [C2] Walk-through completed — no anomalies.
3. [C3] `[POS SYSTEM]` and `[SPA RESERVATION SYSTEM]` powered on and logged in.
4. [C4] Day's schedule printed and reviewed; VIP / special flags noted.
5. [C5] All heating equipment switched on.
6. [C6] Welcome beverages and cold towels prepared.
7. [C7] Locker rooms inspected and stocked.
8. [C8] Surfaces disinfected.
9. [C9] "Spa Open" status communicated.
</Accordion>


<Accordion title="Brand Notes" icon="building">
Properties with extended spa hours or 24-hour spa access may require a modified opening sequence. Document any deviations in a property-specific addendum.
</Accordion>
