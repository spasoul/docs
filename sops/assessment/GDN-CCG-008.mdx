## Purpose

Distribute spa appointments fairly across therapists using a seniority-based round-robin system measured by time (not appointment count), while honoring guest requests, preserving package continuity, and maintaining an auditable scheduling trail.

## Scope

1. [S1] Applies to all spa reception staff and spa coordinators who assign therapists to bookings.
2. [S2] Covers daily appointment distribution, skill-based exceptions, and package continuity rules.
3. [S3] Therapist qualifications and licensing are maintained separately (cross-ref property HR records).

## Responsibility

1. [R1] **Spa Receptionist / Coordinator** — assigns therapists per the rotation rules in this SOP.
2. [R2] **Spa Reception Manager** — audits weekly distribution reports for fairness.
3. [R3] **Spa Director** — resolves disputes and approves exceptions to the rotation.

## Procedure

**Guest-Requested Therapist**

1. [P1] Honor guest-requested therapists first. Appointments assigned by guest request are **excluded from the rotation count** for that day.

**Seniority-Based Round-Robin**

2. [P2] For non-requested appointments, rotate from the most-senior therapist down to the most-junior.
3. [P3] **Time-equivalence rule:** fairness is measured by total treatment time, not appointment count. Two 30-minute treatments equal one 60-minute treatment. Assign accordingly to keep total treatment minutes balanced across the team.

**Skill-Based Exceptions**

4. [P4] If a treatment requires a technique that only one (or a limited subset of) therapist(s) is certified to perform, assign that therapist regardless of rotation position.
5. [P5] Record the skill-based exception in the `[SPA RESERVATION SYSTEM]` booking notes to preserve the audit trail.

**Package Continuity**

6. [P6] For multi-session package treatments, assign the same therapist to all sessions of the package — even if the rotation would otherwise route a session to a different therapist. Continuity takes priority over rotation.

**Rebalancing After Cancellations / No-Shows**

7. [P7] When a cancellation or no-show frees a slot, review the current day's distribution and rebalance the rotation if necessary to maintain fairness. [Cross-ref SOP-GDN-CCG-003]

<Accordion title="Checklist" icon="clipboard-check">
1. [C1] Guest-requested therapists honored and excluded from rotation count.
2. [C2] Non-requested appointments follow seniority round-robin.
3. [C3] Time-equivalence applied (minutes, not appointment count).
4. [C4] Skill-based exceptions documented in booking notes.
5. [C5] Package continuity preserved (same therapist across sessions).
6. [C6] Rotation rebalanced after cancellations/no-shows.
</Accordion>


<Accordion title="Brand Notes" icon="building">
Properties with very small therapist teams (fewer than 4) may simplify the rotation to a basic alternating schedule. The time-equivalence and package-continuity rules still apply.
