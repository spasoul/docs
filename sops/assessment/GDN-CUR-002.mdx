## Purpose

Standardize the in-treatment-room orientation so every guest receives the same greeting, intake, consent, and comfort confirmation before any treatment begins — ensuring the guest feels informed, safe, and in control.

## Scope

1. [S1] Applies to all therapists performing treatments.
2. [S2] Covers the sequence from collecting the guest in the Relaxation Lounge through the moment the treatment begins.
3. [S3] Draping procedures are covered in SOP-SVC-SPA-002. This SOP references that procedure at the appropriate step.

## Responsibility

1. [R1] **Therapist** — executes the full orientation sequence for every guest.
2. [R2] **Spa Supervisor** — monitors orientation quality through periodic observation and guest feedback.

## Procedure

**Guest Collection**

1. [P1] Greet the guest by name in the designated waiting area at exactly the scheduled time, or a few minutes before. [LQA 21.16, 21.18]
2. [P2] Introduce yourself by first name. [LQA 21.18]
3. [P3] Escort the guest to the treatment room, walking slightly ahead at the guest's pace.

**Treatment Confirmation & Health Review**

4. [P4] Reconfirm the treatment booked and the duration aloud. [LQA 21.19]
5. [P5] Review the consultation/health questionnaire with the guest before any product or pressure decision. Carry out a personal health consultation or review the guest's health form, where applicable. [LQA 21.20]

**Treatment Explanation**

6. [P6] Explain the treatment flow: positioning, draping technique, what the guest will feel, and when the therapist will leave and return the room.
7. [P7] Show the guest where to place their belongings, where the call button is, and how to signal for any adjustment during the treatment.

**Privacy & Disrobing**

8. [P8] The therapist must not stay in the room while the guest disrobes. [LQA 21.22]
9. [P9] Knock before re-entering the treatment room. Wait for the guest's verbal acknowledgment before opening the door.

**Comfort Confirmation**

10. [P10] Confirm the guest is properly draped and positioned before starting any technique. [Cross-ref SOP-SVC-SPA-002]
11. [P11] Ensure the guest is comfortable and that the room temperature is acceptable before commencing the treatment. [LQA 21.24]

**Conduct Standards**

12. [P12] Avoid any comments concerning the guest's physique, appearance, marital status, or sexual orientation.
13. [P13] Follow proper draping procedures as specified in SOP-SVC-SPA-002.

<Accordion title="Checklist" icon="clipboard-check">
1. [C1] Guest greeted by name at the scheduled time.
2. [C2] Therapist introduced themselves.
3. [C3] Treatment type and duration reconfirmed.
4. [C4] Health form reviewed.
5. [C5] Treatment flow explained (positioning, draping, sensations, exits/entries).
6. [C6] Belongings storage and call button shown.
7. [C7] Therapist left room during disrobing.
8. [C8] Knocked and waited for acknowledgment before re-entering.
9. [C9] Draping and positioning confirmed.
10. [C10] Room temperature and comfort confirmed.
</Accordion>
