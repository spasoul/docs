## Purpose

Protect therapists and guests from sexual harassment by providing a clear, graduated escalation path — from prevention through verbal warning, treatment termination, and incident reporting — with an unambiguous message that therapist safety is the first priority.

## Scope

1. [S1] Applies to all therapists, spa managers, and spa supervisors.
2. [S2] Covers prevention standards, in-treatment response, escalation, and post-incident documentation.
3. [S3] This SOP addresses guest-initiated advances toward therapists. Staff-on-staff or staff-on-guest harassment is governed by the property's general HR harassment policy.

## Responsibility

1. [R1] **Therapist** — maintains professional boundaries, issues the verbal warning, terminates the treatment if behavior continues, and exits the room.
2. [R2] **Spa Manager** — takes over guest management after the therapist exits, ensures the therapist is removed from the situation, and files the incident report.
3. [R3] **[SPA OPERATIONS DIRECTOR] / [HOTEL ASSISTANT GENERAL MANAGER]** — receives the written incident report and determines further action.

## Procedure

**Prevention**

1. [P1] Staff should never involve themselves in sexual conversation at any time.

**Involuntary Physical Response**

2. [P2] If a male guest gets an erection during a treatment: ensure the area is draped — using more than one towel if necessary — and continue the treatment in a professional manner.

**Verbal Advance — Warning**

3. [P3] If the guest makes a verbal advance, the therapist gives a firm warning that the behavior is not acceptable and the treatment will be terminated if it continues.

**Continued Behavior — Termination**

4. [P4] If the behavior continues, the therapist terminates the treatment, leaves the room, and immediately notifies the manager.
5. [P5] DO NOT CONTINUE TO HAVE ANY CONTACT WITH THE GUEST.
6. [P6] YOUR SAFETY IS MOST IMPORTANT.

**Manager Takeover**

7. [P7] The manager handles the guest from that point forward. The therapist is removed from the situation entirely.

**Incident Report**

8. [P8] A written incident report is filed and shared with the [SPA OPERATIONS DIRECTOR] and the [HOTEL ASSISTANT GENERAL MANAGER].

<Accordion title="Checklist" icon="clipboard-check">
1. [C1] No sexual conversation initiated by staff at any time.
2. [C2] Involuntary response handled with additional draping; treatment continued professionally.
3. [C3] Verbal warning delivered on first advance.
4. [C4] Treatment terminated and room exited on continued behavior.
5. [C5] No further contact between therapist and guest after termination.
6. [C6] Manager assumed guest management.
7. [C7] Written incident report filed and routed to senior management.
</Accordion>
