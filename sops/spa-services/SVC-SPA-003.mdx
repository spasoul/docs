## Purpose

Standardize the post-treatment sequence — from treatment completion through satisfaction check, aftercare advice, beverage service, escort, and farewell — ensuring every guest leaves feeling cared for and informed, with a positive final impression.

## Scope

1. [S1] Applies to all therapists and spa attendants involved in the post-treatment guest experience.
2. [S2] Covers the sequence from the final moment of active treatment through the guest's departure from the spa.
3. [S3] Billing is covered in SOP-GDN-CCG-006. This SOP ends at the escort to reception or the locker room.

## Responsibility

1. [R1] **Therapist** — executes the satisfaction check, aftercare advice, and initial escort.
2. [R2] **Spa Attendant** — prepares and serves post-treatment beverages in the relaxation area.
3. [R3] **Spa Receptionist** — completes billing and delivers the final farewell. [Cross-ref SOP-GDN-CCG-006]

## Procedure

**Treatment Completion**

1. [P1] Gently signal the end of the treatment verbally. Allow the guest a moment to reorient.
2. [P2] Wash hands visibly after the treatment. [LQA 21.23 — cross-ref SOP-SVC-SPA-001 P.3]

**Satisfaction & Aftercare**

3. [P3] Ascertain the guest's satisfaction with the treatment. [LQA 21.32]
4. [P4] Provide post-treatment advice appropriate to the treatment received (e.g., drink plenty of water, skin care advice, avoid sun exposure, rest period). [LQA 21.32]
5. [P5] Confirm that the treatment received reflected what was advertised and booked. [LQA 21.37]

**Privacy for Dressing**

6. [P6] Leave the room to allow the guest to dress in privacy. Knock and wait for verbal acknowledgment before re-entering.

**Beverage Service**

7. [P7] Offer a choice of beverage upon completion of the treatment, or ensure beverages are available in the relaxation room. [LQA 21.33]
8. [P8] All food and beverage items must be offered in non-plastic packaging/containers. [LQA 21.34]
9. [P9] Promote local mineral water or hotel filtered bottled water. [LQA 21.35]

**Escort & Environment**

10. [P10] Escort the guest back to the relaxation lounge, changing/locker room, or reception area. [LQA 21.36]
11. [P11] The treatment room and relaxation room must be clean and well presented with all fresh linen/towels clean and in excellent condition. [LQA 21.38]

**Farewell**

12. [P12] Thank the guest sincerely. Offer a sincere farewell at the end of the interaction and show appreciation. [LQA 21.40]
13. [P13] If appropriate, suggest rebooking or recommend complementary treatments for future visits.

<Accordion title="Checklist" icon="clipboard-check">
1. [C1] Guest satisfaction ascertained.
2. [C2] Post-treatment advice provided.
3. [C3] Treatment matched advertised description.
4. [C4] Privacy given for dressing.
5. [C5] Beverage offered (non-plastic containers).
6. [C6] Local/filtered water promoted.
7. [C7] Guest escorted to lounge, locker room, or reception.
8. [C8] Treatment room and relaxation room clean and well presented.
9. [C9] Sincere farewell and appreciation expressed.
</Accordion>
