## Purpose

Define which spa and fitness services minors may access, under what supervision, with what consent paperwork, and for how long consent records are retained — ensuring legal compliance, child safety, and a clear decision framework for reception and therapist staff.

## Scope

1. [S1] Applies to all spa reception staff, therapists, spa managers, and fitness center attendants.
2. [S2] Covers age-tier rules for both the spa fitness center and spa treatments, consent form requirements, and record retention.
3. [S3] A minor is defined as any guest under 18 years of age.

## Responsibility

1. [R1] **Spa Receptionist** — verifies age, collects signed consent forms, and books only permitted services per the age-tier matrix.
2. [R2] **Therapist / Fitness Attendant** — verifies consent form is on file before service delivery; ensures guardian presence and clothing rules are met.
3. [R3] **Spa Director** — maintains the consent form archive and handles exceptions or disputes.

## Procedure

**Spa Fitness Center — Age-Tier Rules**

1. [P1] Under 18: no access to sauna, steam room, or hammam.
2. [P2] Ages 16–17: gym access permitted only with a parental/guardian authorization form on file.
3. [P3] Ages 15 and under: no fitness center access.

**Spa Treatments — Age-Tier Rules**

4. [P4] Under 18: no body treatments, no massages.
5. [P5] Ages 16–17: facials and waxing (eye, lip, chin only) permitted with a signed consent form. Manicures, pedicures, makeover, hair styling, temporary tattoos, and hair cutting are permitted.
6. [P6] Ages 15 and under: only manicures, pedicures, makeover, hair styling, temporary tattoos, and hair cutting. Guardian must be present and the minor must remain fully clothed throughout.

**Guardian Presence & Clothing**

7. [P7] A parent or guardian must be present if the service is delivered in an enclosed area, such as a spa or treatment room. The minor must remain fully clothed at all times (strapless garment is acceptable).

**Consent Forms**

8. [P8] One consent form may be signed for the use of the spa fitness center and spa services combined.
9. [P9] The signed consent form must be collected before any service begins. Do not proceed without a completed form.

**Record Retention**

10. [P10] Spa management must maintain copies of all consent forms for two (2) years from the date of service.

3. [C3] Signed consent form collected before service.
