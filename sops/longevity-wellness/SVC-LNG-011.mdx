## Purpose

To accurately measure and track body composition, metabolic rate, cardiovascular fitness, and functional performance markers as objective inputs to programme design and progress tracking, using validated diagnostic equipment.

---

## Scope

Covers: DEXA scan (or BIA), VO2 max testing, resting metabolic rate (RMR) assessment, grip strength, and functional movement screening. Imaging diagnostics (MRI, CT) are clinical procedures outside this SOP's scope.

---

## Responsibility

[Specialist] — operates diagnostic equipment, positions guest, and records raw data. [Physician] — reviews and interprets results; provides clinical context; identifies any incidental findings requiring medical referral.

---

## Absolute Contraindications

- **DEXA**: Pregnancy; recent nuclear medicine scan (radioisotope interference); inability to lie still for scan duration
- **VO2 Max (maximal protocol)**: Uncontrolled cardiovascular disease, recent cardiac event, uncontrolled hypertension, active musculoskeletal injury preventing exercise
- **BIA**: Implanted electronic devices (pacemaker, ICD) — electrical current contraindication; pregnancy

## Relative Contraindications (physician clearance required)

- Controlled cardiovascular disease (sub-maximal VO2 protocol appropriate)
- Lymphoedema (BIA unreliable; DEXA preferred)
- Metallic implants in scan field (DEXA — may affect localised reading)
- Recent vigorous exercise, caffeine, or large meal within [X hours] (BIA accuracy affected)

## MEVZUAT Note

> **Tıbbi Cihaz Yönetmeliği** (Turkish Medical Devices Regulation): Article 5 and 10 require diagnostic equipment (DEXA, metabolic testing devices) to meet CE/MDR certification; Art. 83-86 (Piyasa gözetimi ve denetimi — Market surveillance and adverse event reporting) apply and Turkish Ministry of Health ÜTS (Ürün Takip Sistemi) registration; DEXA operators require radiation safety certification from Turkish Atomic Energy Authority (TAEK). **Law No. 1219** (Turkish Medical Practice Law): Article 1 requires physician referral pathway; physician supervision required for exercise stress testing. **KVKK Law No. 6698** (Personal Data Protection Law): Article 6 governs diagnostic data storage and access controls — special category health data protections apply. **Law No. 6331** (Occupational Health and Safety Law): Articles 4, 10, and 17 (Çalışanların eğitimi — Worker Training) mandate facility safety and operator/guest safety during diagnostic procedures.
