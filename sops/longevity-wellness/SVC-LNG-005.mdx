## Purpose

To safely deliver pulsed electromagnetic field therapy sessions to support cellular repair, bone density, inflammation reduction, and pain relief, using calibrated PEMF devices at prescribed frequencies and intensities.

---

## Scope

Covers mat-based and localised PEMF device sessions for wellness applications. High-intensity PEMF devices (rTMS class) require separate clinical protocol.

---

## Responsibility

[Specialist] — certified PEMF operator present for setup and monitoring. Guest may complete session with periodic check-ins; operator remains within call distance.

---

## Absolute Contraindications

- Electronic implants: pacemaker, implanted defibrillator (ICD), cochlear implant, insulin pump, or any active electronic implant
- Pregnancy
- Active bleeding or haemorrhage
- Malignant tumours in or near treatment area
- Epilepsy (high-intensity PEMF — physician clearance required even for low-intensity)

## Relative Contraindications (physician clearance required)

- Metal implants in treatment area (orthopaedic pins, plates — low-intensity PEMF may be appropriate; physician decision)
- History of seizures (stable)
- Organ transplant recipients (immunosuppression interaction — physician review)
- Joint replacements (device-specific guidance required)

## MEVZUAT Note

> **Tıbbi Cihaz Yönetmeliği** (Turkish Medical Devices Regulation): Article 5 and 10 require PEMF devices to meet CE/MDR certification; Art. 83-86 (Piyasa gözetimi ve denetimi — Market surveillance and adverse event reporting) apply and Turkish Ministry of Health ÜTS (Ürün Takip Sistemi) registration. **Law No. 1219** (Turkish Medical Practice Law): Article 1 requires physician referral pathway for guest access to PEMF. **KVKK Law No. 6698** (Personal Data Protection Law): Article 6 governs session records and adverse event documentation. **Law No. 6331** (Occupational Health and Safety Law): Articles 4, 10, 15 (Sağlık gözetimi — Health Surveillance), and 17 (Çalışanların eğitimi — Worker Training) mandate facility safety and operator safety protocols for electromagnetic field equipment.
