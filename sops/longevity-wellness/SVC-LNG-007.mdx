## Purpose

To safely deliver infrared sauna sessions for detoxification support, cardiovascular conditioning, pain relief, and relaxation, using far, mid, or near-infrared spectra at therapeutic temperatures, while preventing heat-related adverse events.

---

## Scope

Covers far-infrared (FIR), mid-infrared (MIR), and near-infrared (NIR) cabin sessions. Does not cover traditional steam saunas or outdoor sauna structures (separate protocols apply).

---

## Responsibility

[Specialist] — briefs guest, monitors safety, and responds to any adverse events. Guest may be unattended inside cabin; operator checks in at prescribed intervals.

---

## Absolute Contraindications

- Severe cardiovascular disease or unstable angina
- Recent myocardial infarction (within [X months] — physician clearance required)
- Uncontrolled hypertension
- Active fever or acute infection
- Pregnancy (first trimester; second/third trimester — physician clearance)
- Implanted electronic devices (pacemaker, ICD — heat and electromagnetic emission risk)
- Multiple sclerosis (heat may exacerbate symptoms — physician decision)
- Active malignancy (heat may stimulate tumour metabolism)

## Relative Contraindications (physician clearance required)

- Controlled hypertension (monitor BP pre- and post-session)
- Adrenal insufficiency
- Haemophilia or bleeding disorders
- Metal joint implants (localised heat may be uncomfortable)
- Medications affecting thermoregulation (diuretics, beta-blockers, anticholinergics)

## MEVZUAT Note

> **Tıbbi Cihaz Yönetmeliği** (Turkish Medical Devices Regulation): Article 5 and 10 require infrared sauna cabins claiming medical benefits to meet CE/MDR certification; Art. 83-86 (Piyasa gözetimi ve denetimi — Market surveillance and adverse event reporting) apply and Turkish Ministry of Health ÜTS (Ürün Takip Sistemi) registration. **Law No. 1219** (Turkish Medical Practice Law): Article 1 requires physician referral pathway for guest access to infrared sauna. **KVKK Law No. 6698** (Personal Data Protection Law): Article 6 governs session records and adverse event documentation. **Law No. 6331** (Occupational Health and Safety Law): Articles 4, 10, and 17 (Çalışanların eğitimi — Worker Training) mandate facility safety, thermal environment hygiene standards, and electrical safety compliance.
