## Purpose

To safely deliver neurofeedback (EEG-based brainwave training) and biofeedback (HRV, EMG, skin conductance, temperature) sessions to support stress regulation, cognitive performance, sleep quality, and autonomic nervous system balance.

---

## Scope

Covers EEG neurofeedback (amplitude and coherence training), HRV biofeedback, EMG biofeedback, and multi-modal biofeedback sessions. Does not cover clinical neurofeedback for diagnosed neurological or psychiatric conditions (separate clinical protocol required).

---

## Responsibility

[Specialist] — certified practitioner; manages electrode/sensor placement, session protocol, and real-time monitoring. Physician referral required if neurological or psychiatric condition identified during screening.


<Warning>
**Absolute Contraindications** — Do not proceed under any circumstances:
- Active psychosis or acute psychiatric episode
- Seizure disorder without physician clearance (EEG-based neurofeedback may trigger photosensitive responses)
- Implanted electronic neurostimulation devices (DBS, VNS, spinal cord stimulator)
- Active substance intoxication
</Warning>


<Note>
**Relative Contraindications** — Physician clearance required:
- History of seizures (stable, with neurologist clearance)
- Severe depression or bipolar disorder (mood monitoring required)
- ADHD or anxiety disorders on medication (protocol adjustment may be needed)
- Severe migraines (EEG cap pressure may trigger)
</Note>
