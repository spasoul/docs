## Purpose

To design a structured, personalised longevity programme for each guest based on their assessment results, goals, contraindication profile, and available treatment modalities.

---

## Scope

Applies after SOP-GDN-CUR-003 is complete. Covers programme structure, session scheduling, modality sequencing, and guest briefing.

---

## Responsibility

[Physician] approves clinical components and contraindication clearances. [Longevity Specialist] designs the programme sequence and delivers the guest briefing.

## MEVZUAT Note

> **Law No. 1219** (Turkish Medical Practice Law): Article 1 requires physician sign-off on programme design, particularly for clinical interventions and treatment sequencing. **KVKK Law No. 6698** (Personal Data Protection Law): Article 6 governs programme documents and health records — all guest programme files must be stored securely with explicit consent documentation. **Law No. 6331** (Occupational Health and Safety Law): Articles 4 and 10 apply to facility safety protocols and programme supervision.
