## Purpose

To establish a comprehensive baseline of each guest's health status, longevity biomarkers, lifestyle factors, and goals before any therapeutic intervention is prescribed or delivered.

---

## Scope

Applies to every new Longevity guest prior to the first treatment session. Returning guests receive an abbreviated re-assessment per SOP-GDN-CUR-005.

---

## Responsibility

[Physician] conducts clinical components. [Longevity Specialist] completes lifestyle and goal sections. [Receptionist] manages scheduling, consent forms, and data entry per SOP-GDN-CUR-006.

---

## Contraindications to Full Assessment

- Guest in acute medical distress — refer to emergency services immediately (SOP-OPR-SAF-019)
- Active infectious illness — defer assessment until recovery

## MEVZUAT Note

> **Law No. 1219** (Turkish Medical Practice Law): Article 1 requires physician oversight for health assessment and diagnostic decisions. **KVKK Law No. 6698** (Personal Data Protection Law): Article 6 governs collection and processing of sensitive health data — intake questionnaires and health records require explicit written consent; Article 10 (Aydınlatma yükümlülüğü — Transparency obligation: inform guest of data controller identity, purpose, recipients, and rights at first contact). **Law No. 6331** (Occupational Health and Safety Law): Articles 4, 10, and 17 (Çalışanların eğitimi — Worker Training) mandate safe assessment environments and emergency preparedness in facility management.
