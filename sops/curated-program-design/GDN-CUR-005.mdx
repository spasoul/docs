## Purpose

To measure progress against baseline biomarkers and goals at defined programme milestones, and to adjust the longevity programme based on results.

---

## Scope

Applies at each scheduled review point within a guest programme. Minimum review frequency: [every X weeks] for residential guests; [at programme completion] for day/short-stay guests.

---

## Responsibility

## MEVZUAT Note

> **Law No. 1219** (Turkish Medical Practice Law): Article 1 requires physician review of biomarker re-assessment and approval of any clinical programme modifications. **KVKK Law No. 6698** (Personal Data Protection Law): Article 6 governs biomarker and progress records — all re-assessment data must be stored with full audit trail (timestamp, author ID, reason for change) and explicit consent documentation. **Law No. 6331** (Occupational Health and Safety Law): Articles 4 and 10 apply to facility safety and programme supervision protocols.
