<Update label="2026-04-13" description="v2.0 — Launch" tags={["New", "Update"]}>
  ## SOP Binder 2026 is live

  The complete SOP Binder is now available as a digital reference for all teams. Version 2.0 replaces the previous document format with a searchable, browsable web experience.

  ### New

  - **83 SOPs across 11 parts** — Every standard operating procedure is now a dedicated page, organized from [Arrival & Emotional Intelligence](/sops/arrival-emotional-intelligence/OPR-EMO-001) through [Safety, Security & Operations](/sops/safety-security-operations/OPR-SAF-001). Browse all parts from the [home page](/).
  - **Interactive landing page** — Card-based navigation lets you jump directly to any part. A new reference section explains [SOP codes, step identifiers, and draft status](/) so you can orient yourself quickly.

  ### Updates

  - **Contextual icons on every SOP** — Each procedure now displays an icon matching its subject (e.g., snowflake for cryotherapy, fire for fire safety) so you can identify content at a glance.
  - **Collapsible checklists and brand notes** — Supervisory checklists and brand deployment notes are tucked into accordions, keeping procedures focused on the steps that matter.
  - **Tabs for multi-section procedures** — Behavioral and safety SOPs with multiple sections use tabs so you can switch between them without scrolling.
  - **Steps for sequential procedures** — Clinical and longevity SOPs display numbered steps for clear, sequential guidance.
  - **Warning callouts for contraindications** — Contraindications and critical safety notes are highlighted with dedicated warning banners.
  - **Draft SOPs clearly marked** — All 27 draft SOPs show a warning banner and sidebar tag so you know which procedures are pending final content.
  - **Warm olive/sage design** — A custom palette with IBM Plex Sans typography gives the binder a clean, professional look with dark mode support.
</Update>
